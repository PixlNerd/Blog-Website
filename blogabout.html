ABOUT

Hello, I'm AJ. My coding journey started when I was about nine or ten years old. I remember moving into a brand new house, and the very next day, I opened a box with my new book, Usborne Coding with Scratch. So there I was, just coding away on my mom's computer. And at the time, I thought it was the coolest thing ever. I remember creating a number guessing game, and even a game where I had a pet to take care of.

So I did Scratch for a little while. Eventually though, I took a break from coding. Just stopped for a couple of years maybe, wish I hadn't. But then recently, maybe a year back, I don't remember why, but I just decided I wanted to start coding. So that's what I started to do.

I tried learning many languages, C++, Python, PHP, JavaScript. I even tried learning HTML and CSS. Turns out I only liked Python and of course, HTML and CSS. I realized I liked designing websites, and so I practiced building many websites, and then eventually I started, and finished, this one.

Now when I was younger, I could never make up my mind about what I want to be after college. I wanted to be everything from a veterinarian to a meteorologist to a neuroscientist to a literal rocket scientist. But I had, some time ago, I decided I wanted to go into programming of sorts. My plan after graduating high school is to go to college for a computer science major. My ultimate goal, however, is to earn a PhD.

My career goal after college is to start out as an entry level front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) developer. Then I want to become a machine learning engineer. In this field I would use programming languages like Python and R to create models to train AI to learn and think. My end-goal career is to be a bioinformatician. This is the field in which computer software is created and used to analyze biology data.

Thank you for taking the time to look at this as well as my blog posts and projects. 😊
